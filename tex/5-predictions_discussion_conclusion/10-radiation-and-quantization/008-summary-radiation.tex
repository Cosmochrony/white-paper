\subsection{Summary}
  \label{subsec:summary-radiation-quantization}

  Radiation and quantization arise from interactions between localized
  matter excitations and the~$\chi$ substrate.
  Photon-like events emerge only during interaction-induced reprojection.
  Quantization reflects geometric and topological constraints of the
  relaxation dynamics at the level of projection: discrete energy transfer
  occurs when continuous projective descriptions satisfy the conditions
  required for localized reprojection.
