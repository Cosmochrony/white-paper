\subsection{Toy-Model of Spectral Gravitational Susceptibility}
\label{app:spectral-gravitational-toy-model}

The local relaxation field strength is
$\mathbf{E}_\chi = -\nabla\Phi_\chi$, governed by
\[
  \nabla \cdot \left[
    \epsilon_{\text{spec}}(\mathbf{E}_\chi)\,
    \mathbf{E}_\chi
  \right]
  = 4\pi G_0\,\rho_b,
\]
with spectral permittivity
$\epsilon_{\text{spec}} = 1 + \phi(\mathbf{E}_\chi)$.
The spectral susceptibility interpolates between
$\phi = 0$ ($|\mathbf{E}_\chi| \gg \mathcal{K}_c$, Newtonian) and
$\phi = \mathcal{K}_c/|\mathbf{E}_\chi|$
($|\mathbf{E}_\chi| \ll \mathcal{K}_c$, saturation).

In the low-field limit,
$g_{\mathrm{eff}} \approx \sqrt{G_0 M \mathcal{K}_c}/r$, yielding
$v^4 = G_0 M \mathcal{K}_c$ (Baryonic Tully--Fisher Relation).
