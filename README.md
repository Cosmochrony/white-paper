This paper presents **Cosmochrony**, an exploratory theoretical framework proposing a minimalist and geometric
origin for **time, spacetime, gravitation, quantum phenomena, and cosmological expansion**.

The central idea is that these phenomena emerge from the **irreversible relaxation of a single
continuous scalar field**, denoted **χ (chi)**, rather than being introduced as independent
fundamental ingredients.

# Core Idea

Cosmochrony is built on three key hypotheses:

1. **Time is not fundamental**  
   Time corresponds to the **monotonic, irreversible relaxation** of a scalar geometric quantity χ.

2. **Space is emergent**  
   Spatial separation arises from **relational differences and gradients of χ** once a stable
   geometric regime is reached.

3. **Matter and forces are emergent structures**  
   Particles are **stable topological excitations (solitons)** of χ.  
   Gravitation, quantum behavior, and radiation arise as **collective or effective phenomena**.

In this view:
- spacetime is not a background,
- quantization is not fundamental,
- cosmic expansion is not driven by dark energy.

# The χ Field

- χ is a **real scalar geometric field**
- it is **not defined on spacetime** — spacetime emerges from it
- its evolution is **globally monotonic**
- its local relaxation speed is bounded by **c**, identified with the speed of light

A minimal kinematic constraint governs its evolution:

0 ≤ ∂tχ ≤ c

and an effective evolution law:

∂tχ = c √(1 − |∇χ|² / c²)

# Particles as Solitons

In Cosmochrony:

- particles are **localized, topologically stable configurations of χ**
- mass corresponds to **resistance to χ relaxation**
- spin arises from **topological twists** (e.g. 4π periodicity for fermions)
- antiparticles correspond to **phase-inverted configurations**

This provides a geometric interpretation of:
- particle stability
- discrete particle species
- the spin–statistics connection

# Gravity as Emergent Time Dilation

Gravitation is not a fundamental interaction.

It emerges because localized excitations of χ:
- increase spatial gradients
- locally **slow down χ relaxation**
- induce differential proper-time flow

This reproduces:
- gravitational time dilation
- spacetime curvature
- Schwarzschild geometry (in the weak-field limit)
- gravitational waves as χ modulations

Black holes correspond to regions where:
∂tχ → 0

leading to **temporal and gravitational shadows**.

# Quantum Phenomena Without Collapse

Quantum behavior is not postulated.

Instead:
- wave behavior is fundamental
- quantization is emergent
- the wavefunction is **effective**, not ontological

Key points:
- the Schrödinger equation emerges from small χ fluctuations
- entanglement corresponds to **shared χ configurations**
- Bell correlations arise **without superluminal signaling**
- measurement is a **decoherence process**, not a fundamental collapse

# Cosmology Without Dark Energy

Cosmic expansion arises from the **global relaxation of χ**.

Consequences:
- the scale factor satisfies `a(t) ∝ χ(t)`
- the Hubble parameter follows `H = χ̇ / χ`
- apparent acceleration is cumulative, not driven by Λ
- the Hubble tension emerges naturally
- no inflation is required to explain horizon-scale correlations

Primordial χ fluctuations naturally reproduce:
- near scale-invariant CMB anisotropies
- acoustic peak structure
- correct cosmic age and scales

# Status of the Framework

Cosmochrony is:

- **exploratory**
- **minimalist**
- **conceptual before formal**
- **phenomenologically consistent**

It does **not** claim:
- to replace General Relativity or Quantum Mechanics
- to be mathematically complete
- to be experimentally confirmed

It **does** aim to:
- reduce ontological assumptions
- unify time, geometry, and quantization
- offer testable deviations from ΛCDM and standard gravity
